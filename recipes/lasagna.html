Lasagna

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta sheets, rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese. This hearty and flavorful meal is perfect for family dinners and special occasions.

Our lasagna recipe combines ground beef, tomatoes, garlic, and herbs for a delicious ragu, layered with silky béchamel sauce and baked until golden and bubbling. Serve it hot with a side of fresh salad or garlic bread.

Ingredients

  • For the meat sauce:
    • 500g ground beef
    • 1 medium onion, chopped
    • 2 cloves garlic, minced
    • 1 can (400g) crushed tomatoes
    • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
    • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
    • 1 teaspoon dried basil
    • 1 teaspoon sugar (optional, to balance acidity)
    • Salt and pepper to taste
    • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • For the béchamel sauce:
    • 50g butter
    • 50g all-purpose flour
    • 500ml milk
    • A pinch of nutmeg
    • Salt and pepper to tastes
  • Other ingredients:
    • 12 lasagna noodles (pre-cooked or no-boil)
    • 200g shredded mozzarella cheese
    • 100g grated Parmesan cheese
    • Fresh basil or parsley for garnish (optional)
  1. Prepare the meat sauce:

    Heat olive oil in a pan. Cook chopped onion and garlic until softened. Add ground beef and cook until browned. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 20-30 minutes.

  2. Make the béchamel sauce:

    In a saucepan, melt butter. Add flour and cook for 1-2 minutes. Gradually whisk in the milk, then cook until thickened. Season with salt, pepper, and a pinch of nutmeg.

  3. Assemble the lasagna:

    Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F). In a baking dish, layer meat sauce, lasagna noodles, béchamel sauce, and mozzarella. Repeat until all ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of béchamel and mozzarella.

  4. Bake:

    Cover with foil and bake for 25-30 minutes. Remove foil and bake for another 10-15 minutes until the top is golden and bubbly.

  5. Serve:

    Let the lasagna rest for 10 minutes before serving. Garnish with Parmesan and fresh herbs, if desired.
